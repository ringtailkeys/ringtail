# Ringtail — Brand Bible

> **Source of truth.** All other brand docs derive from this file.

**Tagline:** He raids the token pages so you don't.

**Slug:** `ringtail`

---

## Theme

**Night Shift** — a warm, nocturnal "cozy-dumpster-at-2am" world where a scrappy trash-panda works the graveyard shift while you sleep: raiding provider dashboards, washing the loot, and stacking your keys into neat stash-pockets. The anti-vault — an open-source dev tool run by one very competent raccoon, not a cold blue-grey enterprise secrets locker. The whole bit is the name: a ringtail is a real raccoon-cousin and its ringed tail IS your keyring. The bandit mask reads "thief," but the joke is inversion — the only bandit you'd trust with every key you own. Sacred green glow = "it worked, you're in."

---

## Positioning

The local, open-source keyring bandit: your coding agent's little raccoon reads `.env.example` as the shopping list, raids every provider's token pages via their official APIs, validates the scopes, and stashes each key into `.env.local` AND Infisical across dev, staging, and prod — one human "allow" per provider, then zero-touch forever.

---

## Mascot — Rocco

- **Species:** Ringtail (trash-panda / raccoon-cousin)
- **Personality:** Scrappy, deadpan, and quietly competent — the loyal night-shift bandit who already raided your token pages last night while you slept. Unbothered, a little smug, never creepy. Peak "he's just a little guy." The only thief you'd trust with every key you own.

| Expression | Meaning |

| **mind-blown** | shiny-eyed, fangs out, keyring tail flaring = launch / big-win moment |

## Is / Is Not

### Is
- A scrappy, local, open-source keyring bandit with a face and a scheme
- Warm and nocturnal — moonlit cream, loot amber, one sacred green
- One human "allow" per provider, then zero-touch forever
- Official-APIs-only: the agent drives real provider APIs, you just click allow
- Sacred green means exactly one thing: it worked / scope validated / synced
- Sticker-native — Rocco is a Discord emoji, a hoodie, a die-cut sticker

### Is Not
- A cold blue-grey enterprise secrets vault or compliance dashboard
- A breathing gradient "AI orb" or glowing blob
- A browser-bot puppeting your login pages
- Corporate "enterprise-grade secrets management" speak
- A rented SaaS that stores your secrets behind someone else's login
- Cutesy or creepy — Rocco is deadpan and competent, never either

## Metaphors

- Ringed tail = your keyring (the whole name is the pun)
- Night raid while you sleep = zero-touch, one-allow-then-autopilot
- Washing the loot before he trusts it = scope validation on every key
- Three stash-pockets = provisioning per environment (dev / staging / prod)
- Bandit-mask inversion = trusted security with a wink — the thief is your bodyguard
- Inspecting a key in the moonlight until it glows green = validate-on-the-spot success
- Reading `.env.example` as tonight's shopping list = the manifest drives the raid

## Competitor Context

The credentials/secrets category is a wall of cold, near-identical blue-grey enterprise tools with no character: HashiCorp Vault, Doppler, Infisical, EnvKey, 1Password. They STORE your secrets behind a login and make you do the soul-crushing acquisition yourself — 15 token pages, wrong scopes, per-env drift. Ringtail rebels on two axes at once: (1) it inverts the job — instead of a vault you fill by hand, a competent little bandit GOES AND GETS the keys for you and writes them to `.env.local` + Infisical; and (2) it's warm, mascot-led, and sticker-native — the hard anti-orb in a sea of gradient blobs and slate dashboards. Name has low collision in the dev space (Vault/Bandit/Stash are all taken; a real raccoon-cousin whose tail = keyring is genuinely ownable). Confidence high on differentiation and virality; moderate on exact `.com`/`.dev` availability (`ringtail.dev` / `getringtail.com` the realistic homes).
